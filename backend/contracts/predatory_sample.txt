Sponsorship Agreement

1. Exclusivity: The Creator agrees to be exclusive to the Brand for a period of 12 months from the date of signing. The Creator shall not promote, mention, or collaborate with any other beauty, skincare, or wellness brand across all social media platforms during this period.

2. Content Ownership: The Brand shall own all right, title, and interest in and to the content created under this agreement in perpetuity. This includes an irrevocable, worldwide, perpetual license to use the Creator's name, image, and likeness in any and all media.

3. Payment Terms: Payment shall be made within 90 days after the end of the campaign (Net-90).

4. Revisions: The Brand may request unlimited revisions to any content until the Brand is fully satisfied.

5. Termination: The Brand may terminate this agreement at any time for any reason. No kill fee shall be paid if the Brand cancels the campaign after the brief has been delivered.

6. Auto-Renewal: This agreement shall automatically renew for successive 12-month periods unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal at least 90 days prior to the end of the current term.

7. Governing Law: This agreement is governed by the laws of the state of Delaware, USA.
