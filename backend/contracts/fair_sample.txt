Sponsorship Agreement

1. Exclusivity: The Creator agrees to be exclusive to the Brand for a period of 60 days from the date of publication. Exclusivity is limited to direct competitors in the skincare category.

2. Content Rights: The Brand is granted a non-exclusive license to use the content for 12 months from the date of publication. After this period, all rights revert to the Creator.

3. Payment Terms: Payment shall be made within 30 days of the invoice date (Net-30).

4. Revisions: The Brand is entitled to two rounds of revisions per deliverable. Additional revisions will be charged at a rate of ₹5,000 per round.

5. Termination: Either party may terminate this agreement with 14 days notice. If the Brand cancels after the content has been shot, a kill fee of 30% of the total deal value shall be paid.

6. Governing Law: This agreement is governed by the laws of India.
